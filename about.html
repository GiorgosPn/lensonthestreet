About

Through my lens, I try to hold onto what quietly drifts away: a glance, a gesture, a moment suspended between presence and absence. Each photograph is less a record of appearances and more a trace of emotion, an imprint of connection amid the crowd, a silent thread between strangers.

I am a street photographer based in Athens, Greece. My work is rooted in observation of everyday urban life, seeking to reveal fleeting intersections of light, shadow, and human presence. Over the years, I have attended numerous workshops and seminars in creative and conceptual photography — experiences that shaped my visual language and deepened my focus on storytelling through the street.
